// STEP 5 OF 6 — STAT ALLOCATION
ABILITY SCORES
"The slums either made you strong or made you clever. Usually not both."
Point Buy — 27 Points
Each stat starts at 8. Spend 27 points to raise them. 8–13 costs 1 point each. 14 costs 2 extra. 15 costs 2 extra. Maximum is 15 before racial bonuses. Modifier = (score − 10) ÷ 2, rounded down. This is what actually affects rolls.
■ FIRST TIME? Don't try to be good at everything. Pick 2 stats your class lives on and push them high. The rest can stay low — Lythern will punish your weaknesses regardless, so you'd rather have real strengths than mediocre everything. Your class tip below tells you exactly what to prioritise. Use it. Or don't, and learn the hard way.
